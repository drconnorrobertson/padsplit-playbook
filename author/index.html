The Author

Dr. Connor Robertson

Author, advisor, and operator working at the intersection of small-business strategy and affordable housing.

Connor runs an active tax advisory practice, writes daily about real estate and business, and operates a portfolio of shared-housing properties on the PadSplit platform. He writes for the operator — the person actually doing the work — and his books are designed to be the manuals he wishes he'd had when he started.

Background

Connor's professional path has crossed clinical practice, business consulting, tax advisory, real estate operations, and publishing. Across all of it, the thread has been the same: building useful systems for working professionals — and writing them down so other people can use them.

As an advisor, Connor works with small business owners on tax strategy, entity structure, and growth planning. As an operator, he runs and consults on shared-housing properties. As a writer, he publishes daily — long-form, technical, and operationally specific.

Writing Philosophy

Most business books are aspirational. Connor's are operational. The goal is always the same: write the manual that the reader can put on the desk and work from. No filler, no chest-pounding, no fluff. Just the system, the math, the templates, and the lessons learned at cost.

If you've read his work and want to get in touch, the best ways are through the contact page or any of the properties above.
